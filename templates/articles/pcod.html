Understanding PCOD

Polycystic Ovarian Disease (PCOD) is a common hormonal disorder that affects millions of women worldwide. It occurs when the ovaries produce an excessive number of immature eggs, leading to cyst formation and hormonal imbalances.

Common Symptoms of PCOD:
  • Irregular or absent menstrual cycles
  • Unwanted hair growth on face and body
  • Weight gain and difficulty losing weight
  • Acne, oily skin, and hair thinning
  • Mood swings, anxiety, and depression
  • Difficulty conceiving (infertility)
