Understanding Thyroid Disorders

  • Hypothyroidism: A condition where the thyroid is underactive, leading to fatigue, weight gain, hair loss, and depression.
  • Hyperthyroidism: An overactive thyroid that causes weight loss, anxiety, rapid heartbeat, and excessive sweating.
  • Hashimoto’s Thyroiditis: An autoimmune condition where the immune system attacks the thyroid.
  • Goiter: An abnormal enlargement of the thyroid gland.
